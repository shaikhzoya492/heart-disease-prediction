HEART FAILURE

It occurs when the heart is unable to pump blood effectively, leading to inadequate oxygen supply to the body. It can result from conditions like coronary artery disease, high blood pressure, or previous heart attacks.Early detection and treatment can improve quality of life and prevent further complications.

HEART ATTACK

It occurs when blood flow to a part of the heart is blocked, causing damage to the heart muscle. It can result from clogged arteries due to plaque buildup or other heart-related conditions.Quick medical intervention is crucial to prevent severe damage and improve survival chances.
